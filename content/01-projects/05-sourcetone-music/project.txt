Text: As a music lover, this was a really enjoyable project to be a part of. Sourcetone came to us having their own development already ongoing in multiple realms but wanted to consider how it could reach into the mobile space.

The technologies we based the app design from were researched by our client's board of very talented and well-studied music specialists. They requested a set of important features they needed and gave us the opportunity to consider ways the technology could be interacted with by users and how those features might be made concrete.
