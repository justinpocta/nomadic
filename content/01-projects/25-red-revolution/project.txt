Text:
The Red Revolution was a personal project of a good friend of mine, Adam Thomason. With a background in fashion and a heart to do something beyond simply making and selling a product, he launched an initiative to merge fashion sense with a social consciousness. We were able to piece together his branding and product with a photoshoot, simple one page design to feature the product, and Twitter feed to enable a news stream.
